# Incident Response Policy

## Purpose

Define how Prymal detects, triages, contains, recovers from, and learns from security and availability incidents.

## Scope

Security incidents, production outages, suspected data exposure, payment/auth incidents, and major supplier failures affecting Prymal.

## Owner

Founder / Security lead

## Review Frequency

Annually and after major incidents.

## Requirements

- Incidents must be logged with date, severity, impact, and owner.
- High-impact incidents require containment, recovery, and corrective-action tracking.
- Potential secret exposure requires credential rotation assessment.
- Evidence such as logs, timelines, and screenshots should be preserved.
- Incident response drills should be performed at least annually.

## Evidence Expected

- incident register
- incident drill output
- corrective action register
- management review notes

## Exceptions Process

Any deviation from the incident process must be documented in the incident record with justification and follow-up action.
